Takeaways

Patience

Reading Odyssey has taught me how to deal with children that are not the most obedient. When I explain some math concepts with my friend, I will go through step by step and make sure he does not have any doubts or misconceptions.

Self Control

While preparing for my examinations, I realised that I had constantly lacked the motivation to continue, and would often ended up being distracted. I realised if I had focused on my actions and only that of the present moment, I could gain an deeper awareness of my actions and enter the state of flow more easily.

Teamwork

I learnt to accept suggestions and criticism from my classmates and use them to improve on myself.

PERMA+ Model

At HCIBS, there was a good environment to foster good habits, like sleeping earlier. I used this chance at IBP to form good habits, such as sleeping earlier, waking up on time, etc.

Simply meeting my friends is where I find meaning in coming to school. I believe the inherent value comes from the nuances in school life, e.g. taking notes, saying words of appreciation to the canteen vendors, etc.

Positive Emotions: I gain satisfaction not from my achievements, but from knowing that I put in extreme amounts of hard work to attain such a goal. Being constantly grateful also allows me to appreciate the subtleties around me.
