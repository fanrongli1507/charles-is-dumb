学而不思则罔，思而不学则殆

English Definition: Learning without thought is labor lost; thought without learning is knowledge lost. Learn for the sake of learning, not in a perfunctory manner.

天行健，君子以自强不息

A true man shall never stop progressing, like the stars. Here, the derived meaning would be to focus and persevere, even when faced with the most mundane tasks.
